Portfolio

The Blackhole

The 3D artwork I created is an artistic visualization of a black hole with an accretion disk surrounding it. At its center, the dark and mysterious black hole creates gravitational distortion, visible through the effect of light bending. The accretion disk, made of brightly glowing gas and dust, circles at high speed, emitting intense energy.

The Galaxies

The 3D artwork I created depicts an outer space scene with a black background filled with stars. There are three spiral galaxies visible in this image. The largest galaxy at the bottom center features a stunning blend of blue and purple colors, with gently swirling spiral arms. Above it, there are two smaller galaxies; one has a soft brown hue, while the other is bright purple with a striking black core. The beauty and diversity of colors from these galaxies create a magical and mysterious impression of the vast universe.

Guardians of the Sea and Sky

The 3D artwork I created is a stunning scene of a naval fleet at sunset, with a backdrop of rocky mountains shrouded in mist. In the foreground, a formidable aircraft carrier is flanked by two large warships, each ready and alert for action. The carrier's deck is filled with fighter jets, showcasing its immense power. Above, three fighter jets soar across the sky, their sleek silhouettes clearly visible against the golden hues of dusk.

Edge of Light

The 3D artwork I created depicts the Earth in the vast, silent expanse of space. Half of the planet is illuminated by sunlight, while the other half is shrouded in the shadow of night. A thin line between day and night seems to divide the planet, creating an eternal harmony. Among the sparkling stars, Earth may appear small, but it is where life breathes, full of countless untold stories.

The Fish

The 3D artwork I created features a Moorish Idol fish (Zanclus cornutus), known for its distinctive yellow, black, and white coloration. This fish has a flat body shape with a long, prominent dorsal fin and a small, pointed mouth. The plain teal background highlights the details and vibrant colors of the fish.
